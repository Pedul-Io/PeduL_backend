Want to keep up with us?

At PeduL, our mission is to alleviate the pressure of tuition prices and educational costs for the average student, and to provide potential donors with a medium through which they can benefit. PeduL will give students a practical way to raise money for their education.

Our vision is to provide students with an intuitively designed, easy-to-use website through which they can showcase their academic and personal merit, as well as a place for desiring donors to actively support the college pursuits of said individuals.
